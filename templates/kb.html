How to Connect to SchoolNet WiFi
🌐 NETWORK By Admin · 145 views · Published Mar 1, 2025

Follow these steps to connect your device to the school's wireless network SchoolNet:

  1. Click the WiFi icon on your system taskbar (bottom-right on Windows, top-right on Mac).
  2. Select SchoolNet from the list of available networks.
  3. When prompted, enter your school email as the username and your account password.
  4. Click Connect. Your device will authenticate and connect within a few seconds.
  5. If prompted about a security certificate, click Accept / Trust.

⚠ If you continue to experience connection issues, please submit a support ticket with your device type and error message.
